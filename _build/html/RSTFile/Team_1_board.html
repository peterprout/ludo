Team_1_board module¶

class Team_1_board.Board(my_player, all_pieces, colour_to_img)[source]¶

Bases: object

Draws the main Ludo board and the moving pieces.

It also checks if two pieces are on the same spot. If that is the case, then the size of the pieces is changed. It also checks if a piece has killed and opponents piece.

Parameters:
  • my_player (instance object) – Your player object.
  • all_pieces (list) – All the pieces on the board.
  • colour_to_img (dict) – Dictionary that map player colour to images.

check_conflict(moving_piece)[source]¶

Checks if two pieces are in the same position.

Goes through all the pieces on the board and checks if there is a conflict. If there is and the opponents piece is not in a safe spot then kill the piece by calling the death_function. Also call the check_many_pieces to check if there are other pieces that are in conflicting positions.

Parameters:moving_piece (instance object) – instance of moving piece

draw_arrows_and_stars()[source]¶

Draws the arrows and stars on the board. It also calls the draw_boxes function. The arrows are drawn using pygame’s draw function. The arrow images are blit on to the board.

draw_board(check)[source]¶

Draws the main board.

It calls the draw_arrows_and_stars function. It also draws the rectangle and the circles as home bases for the pieces.

It then draw the pieces, genie, roll button and displays dice.

Parameters:check (int) – Indicates if the player and their pieces are flashing.
draw_boxes()[source]¶

Draws rectangles for each position on the board using the boc_pos list. It also blits star images on the board.

draw_genie()[source]¶

Draws the genie on the board depending on who has the genie. If no-one has the genie, a lamp is placed in the middle.

draw_pieces(check=0)[source]¶

Draws the pieces on the board.

It goes through all the pieces on the board and draw it in their position. If there is a conflict their size changes. The pieces flash is they are movable depending on the dice roll.

move_piece(piece_num, step)[source]¶

This moves the piece assuming the piece is movable.

If the piece is in the home position, it can onl be moved if a 6 is rolled on the dice. It also draws the pieces as they move each step. After the piece has move it checks if there is a conflict with another piece on the board.

Parameters:
  • piece_num (int) – Number in index of ALL_PIECE of this piece
  • step (int) – Number of steps/moves by this piece
